{{< about-intro >}}
I’m a UX/UI Designer and Design Engineer based in Milan.
I’ve worked on digital products, games, and websites, with a strong focus on structure, systems, and complex user flows.
I’m less interested in visual polish and more in how things behave over time.
That’s where I do my best work.
{{< /about-intro >}}

{{< accordion id="about-faq" >}}

{{< accordion-item title="What do you work on?" >}}
I work on complex applications rather than showcase websites. I’m comfortable with products that have rules, states, and evolving requirements, where clarity comes from structure instead of visual emphasis.
{{< /accordion-item >}}

{{< accordion-item title="How do you approach design?" >}}
I approach design as a system. I focus on relationships, constraints, and consistency, trying to understand how decisions affect the product as it grows.
{{< /accordion-item >}}

{{< accordion-item title="What are you not focused on?" >}}
I don’t focus on graphic production such as illustrations, 3D modeling, animations, or posters. When a project needs that kind of visual work, I collaborate with graphic designers or other artists. My focus remains on interface design and system structure.
{{< /accordion-item >}}

{{< accordion-item title="Do you work close to code?" >}}
Yes. I collaborate closely with developers and care about how interfaces are implemented, scaled, and maintained. This helps keep design decisions grounded in reality.
{{< /accordion-item >}}

{{< accordion-item title="What kind of projects are a good fit?" >}}
Projects that deal with complexity seriously and value long term clarity. Applications, tools, and products that need to remain understandable as they evolve.
{{< /accordion-item >}}
